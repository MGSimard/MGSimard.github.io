The Mage - Myth War Online

The Unrelenting Flame of Mage Town

Specialized Skills

  • Fire (I-IV) - Engulf your target(s) in burning flames, dealing massive damage.
    • Fire I - Single target
    • Fire II - Four targets
    • Fire III - Single target, increased damage
    • Fire IV - Six targets
  • Dark (I-IV) - Channel the forces of chaos, heavily damaging all targets.
    • Dark I - Single target
    • Dark II - Four targets
    • Dark III - Single target, increased damage
    • Dark IV - Five targets
  • Flash (I-IV) - Hard hitting light energy spell, penetrates resistances.
    • Flash I - Single target
    • Flash II - Four targets
    • Flash III - Single target, increased damage
    • Flash IV - Five targets
  • Death (I-IV) - On successful cast, executes a target after a certain amount of rounds.
    • Death I - Single target, four round delay
    • Death II - Four targets, five round delay
    • Death III - Single target, three round delay
    • Death IV - Five targets, five round delay
